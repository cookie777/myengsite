Plastic is a very versatile and useful material that we use these days. Plastic is tougher than paper and lighter than steel. It is also watertight and flexible. However, there is a huge disadvantage of plastic. It has a sever impact and damage to the environment. White pollution which is caused by the accumulation of abandoned plastic is a serious problem these days. It also jeopardizes our health.
