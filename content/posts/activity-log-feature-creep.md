# A look-back on the day

Feature creep is a tendency that the more a system or a product has features, the more complicated and difficult it becomes to use. Adding a lot of functions or features doesn't always mean it will be better for users or even those who manage the system. The significant point is whenever we update the system or product, we have to keep it simple and sometimes be courageous to reduce some of its features.😵
