# A look-back on the day


I changed my key bindings following to the United States version. Technically, not modified but added additional keybinding. The criterion of the Japanese keyboard has its unique keybindings, which is a little bit different from the US ones. Namely, some symbol such as ' and ", which is frequently used in writing, has a lot of impact on TOEFL. This is because the keybindings used in TOEFL is also based on the US standard.
